Wondering who we are and what we do?

TBS is a place where stories, letters and testimonies of the believers in our Lord Jesus are talked about in our favorite Nigerian language, 'Pidgin English'. These stories are told in a funny and relatable way to grant deeper understanding of the Bible to everyone who reads especially those struggling with understanding English.

And guess what! The fun just got better because we also have other sections like 'Funny interpretations', where we give hilarious meanings to bible verses and 'Words of Wisdom', where we give you an in-depth knowledge of verses in the Bible that seem hard to understand.
